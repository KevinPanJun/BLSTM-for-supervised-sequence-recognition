Hidden Layer size vs Space occupied by network

The space required by the network is the same as the space required to store the weight of each edge

Since total number of weights is W = 4IH + 4H2 + 3H + HK we expect a quadratic dependancy between Number of Hidden Cells and Space

For this experiment we use 15 input features and an output layer of 173 classes

As the image shows, the relationship is quadratic

In the range of 10 to 3000, the size varies from 3Mb to 5.7 Gb. We use 150 hidden cells, which has a size of 50 Mb and after 30 epochs of training becomes 80 Mb
